About BikeRent Pro

Learn more about our company, mission, and the team behind our success.

Our Story

BikeRent Pro was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make high-quality bicycles accessible to everyone. What started as a small shop with just 10 bikes has now grown into a city-wide network with over 200 bicycles available for rent.

Our founder, an avid cyclist, noticed the need for affordable and convenient bike rentals in the city. Today, we're proud to offer a diverse fleet of well-maintained bicycles for all types of riders and adventures.

Our Mission

At BikeRent Pro, we believe in promoting sustainable transportation and healthy lifestyles. Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and convenient bicycle rentals to encourage more people to choose cycling over other forms of transportation.

We're committed to reducing our carbon footprint and helping our customers do the same. Every bike rented from us is one less car on the road, contributing to cleaner air and a healthier environment for everyone.
